Allow me to introduce myself

Thank you for visiting my site! My name is Caleb Herron. I am a percussionist based in Atlanta, GA and a member of Chamber Cartel, which is an up and coming chamber music ensemble that has played numerous shows. Not only do I play music professionally, but I teach as well. My passion for teaching and music has led to to give personal lessons in percussion. If you're interested in hiring me for an event or perhaps you're interested in lessons, feel free to reach out to me! You're welcome to browse around. Thank you again for viewing!
